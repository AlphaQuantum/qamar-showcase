Why choose us

Sugar Free

Our "Sugar Free" energy drink is not just a label, but a reality. We believe in providing consumers with a healthy alternative to traditional energy drinks. Our mission is to satisfy the need for energy without compromising dietary goals, offering the best of both worlds: health and energy.

Caffienated

Caffeine is often referred to as a "miracle drug" due to its significant impact on human societies. Caffeine has been proved to be a source of energy that does not have any side-effects, it has been shown to increase productivity and focus, which are key benefits of energy drinks like ours.

Gluten Free

Our gluten-free drinks cater to the growing demand for healthier alternatives for those with dietary restrictions. As gluten can cause digestive issues for a minority of people we decided to ensure that anyone can enjoy a healthier option with our gluten-free products.

Naturally Flavoured

Savor the essence of nature with our 100% naturally flavored beverages. Our commitment to not using any additives or preservatives ensures that each sip is bursting with authentic, rich flavor. Enjoy the taste of nature at its finest. "As sweet as sweet can get."

Low Calories

Low-calories are the perfect solution for those looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle without sacrificing taste. Our products are carefully concocted to deliver the same delicious flavor, but with fewer calories. Enjoy the benefits of a healthier lifestyle without sacrificing the taste you love.

Protien

Fuel your body with the power of protein. Our products are packed with high-quality protein, providing the essential nutrients your body needs to perform at its best. Whether you're an athlete or just looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle, our protein-rich options are the perfect choice.

Our Origin

In the 1950s, shortly after the end of the second World War, the Ananas Family began brewing their drinks in a small town in italy, soothing the hearts of the families torn by the war.

Originating from Morocco, they brought with them their expertise in brewing Moroccan Atay Tea and Arabian Coffee. As their mention grew on the locals' tongues, they expanded their skills to encompass the towns favourite lime and berry beverages.

Through tireless effort and dedication, we perfected our drinks, which have now become the foundation of the exceptional beverages you enjoy today. Our drinks are pure and natural, crafted with the same commitment to quality and authenticity as the Ananas family's original recipes.
